# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Decision-makers evaluating Emidio Torre for senior data and platform advisory work:

- **Enterprise data leaders** — CDOs, heads of data/platform, VPs of engineering — assessing whether he can lead the design and delivery of an analytics or data platform, drive AI adoption responsibly, or untangle a stalled pipeline/architecture problem.
- **Government & public-sector institutions** — bodies commissioning data platforms, geospatial/GIS systems, and analytics infrastructure, often in an environmental or regulatory context.

They arrive skeptical and time-poor. They are not hiring a freelancer for a website; they are deciding whether to trust someone with a high-stakes, expensive, politically visible data program. The job the site does: establish, within one scroll, that this is a senior operator who has done exactly this before and can do it again quickly and well.

## Product Purpose

A personal site that repositions Emidio Torre from creative/UI-UX developer to **senior business + technical consultant** operating at the intersection of data strategy and delivery.

Competency areas (framed as outcomes, not a skills list): GIS & geospatial data, data visualization, platform engineering, data pipeline & architecture design, AI adoption and AI management/governance, and analytics data platforms. Domain depth in methane / environmental emissions data.

Flagship engagement: consultant to **IMEO (International Methane Emissions Observatory)**, leading development of their Data Platform — named but kept high-level, with no confidential specifics or metrics.

Success = a qualified buyer leaves convinced of both technical depth and business judgment, and reaches out about an engagement.

## Brand Personality

**Precise. Authoritative. Engineered.**

Voice: calm, direct, and confident — a senior operator who states outcomes plainly and doesn't oversell. Technical fluency shown, not claimed. Restraint reads as seniority; spectacle would read as junior.

The previous site's playful register (random color-wheel, "don't click" jokes, marquee, three.js toy hero) is **evolved, not preserved**: interactivity stays only where it reads as *engineered and intentional* — a demonstration of craft, ideally data-driven (a precise geospatial/analytics visualization), never a gimmick.

Emotional goal: trust and reassurance. The visitor should feel "this person has solved harder versions of my problem."

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS / fintech-navy.** Navy-and-gold, gradient hero-metric templates, identical feature-card grids, "trusted by" logo walls as the whole story.
- **The old playful portfolio voice.** Toy interactions, jokey microcopy, decorative motion with no meaning.
- **Monospace-as-costume "developer" aesthetic.** Mono everywhere to signal technical; reads as cliché, not competence.
- **Editorial-magazine default.** Display-serif + italic + drop caps + broadsheet grid on a brief that isn't a magazine.
- **Consultancy-stock blandness.** Handshake photos, abstract blue swooshes, meaningless "digital transformation" imagery.

## Design Principles

- **Show the work, not adjectives.** Credibility comes from problem → approach → outcome narratives and real data-craft, not from claiming "technical excellence."
- **Restraint is the seniority signal.** Confident, quiet, precise beats loud. Every element earns its place; nothing is decorative-by-default.
- **Prove competence by demonstrating it.** The site's own engineering and data visualization are the portfolio — practice what you preach.
- **Facts only, honestly.** State only what's confirmed; mark gaps with visible TODOs. Never invent client outcomes, metrics, or quotes. Integrity is part of the reputation being built.
- **One clear next step.** A time-poor decision-maker should always know how to start a conversation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥3:1; verified, not assumed.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected on every animation with a meaningful non-motion fallback.
- Content legible and fully functional without JavaScript-gated reveals; motion enhances an already-visible default.
- Keyboard-navigable interactive elements; visible focus states.
